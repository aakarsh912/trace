PS2-4

Working hours, Overtime, Attendance Record

Submitted PS2 · Labor MSMeera Sharma · Round 2 of review
Recommendation

a) Eldeco shall ensure compliance with the statutory working hour limits stipulated under the BOCW (Regulation of Employment and Conditions of Service) Act, 1996 and Rules, 2005, and ensure that no worker is engaged beyond the permissible hours.

b) Eldeco shall ensure compliance with the BOCW Act, 1996, by paying workers overtime at twice the normal wage rate, maintaining a proper attendance muster roll, and accurately documenting all working-hour and overtime records as required under the Act.

c) Eldeco shall ensure that wages paid to security personnel are in line with the revised minimum wage notification dated March 2025, applicable from October 2025.

d) Increase the number of security personnel at the proposed Project site and restructure working hours into three shifts of eight hours each, instead of twelve-hour shifts, to ensure compliance with working hours and overtime wages are in compliance with applicable Indian labour laws.
